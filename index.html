Band kids just got 2013 spark design award finalist certificates!
And this design will now go on to be judged in October by the Phase 2 live jury in San Francisco.

We want kids to experience the beauty of music, the novelty of multisensory interaction, and the joy of creation, so, we create a new type of interaction experience that regards operating tangible instruments as a part of children’s creative development process.Children can twist and combine their tangible instruments to create different music effects. In addition, by linking multiple iPads, children can also enjoy the pleasure of co-creation with friends. Interacting with the tangible instruments and iPad allows children to learn to create music and have fun through multisensory interactions, including visual, audio, and tactile interactions. Besides, through the process of collaboration, exploration and combination, tangible instruments not only improve children's interest about music, but also enhance the ability of in-hand manipulation of the ability of in-hand manipulation of young children.

Tangible toys got 2012 YSED 9th application - Sliver Award & Special Award of the enterprise!

we aimed at 3~6 years old children and designed a children’s learning app with tangible toys on the tablet. By extending Frobel and Montessori’s tactile learning theory, we implemented two game based learning applications with tangible toys to bring a novel interactive learning experience for children. We conducted the preliminary study of 4 kindergarten children. The result showed that children were interested in tangible toys and had more fun in the learning activities. Finally, our capacitive tags can be used to design various forms of digital manipulatives on the tablets.
