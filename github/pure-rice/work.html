  • Tangible toys + iPad

    interaction design tangible iPad app

    Let’s give your kids a digital toy memory.

    view project

    Tangible toys + iPad



    we aimed at 3~6 years old children and designed a children’s learning app with tangible toys on the tablet. By extending Frobel and Montessori’s tactile learning theory, we implemented two game based learning applications with tangible toys to bring a novel interactive learning experience for children. We conducted the preliminary study of 4 kindergarten children. The result showed that children were interested in tangible toys and had more fun in the learning activities. Finally, our capacitive tags can be used to design various forms of digital manipulatives on the tablets.

  • Interactive device of visualized social network on facebook

    interaction design Information visualization maya unity3D

    We have designed a social network visualization interactive device with Facebook in this study. The device is named Moldbox. We collect some information from a group of people who know each other in real life, and represent each per with a mold avatar. Each mold avatar has its own hypha linked to another avater, which means they are closest friends in this group.

  • Chinese Characters Recombination

    interaction design chinese culture maya unity3D

    學者把漢字的構成和使用方式歸納成「六書」，其 中會意、形聲使用的「組字法」即是將兩個個別有意義的"文"組合成一個新的"字"。兒童初學字時都是直接把字合起來記憶，因此也失去了把字拆開觀看的視點。樂高亦是另一種關於拆解、蓋建的積木遊戲，我們想把同樣組合的樂趣與漢字的創建結合。透過這件作品，對小孩來說，手動、立體文字與動畫將加深他們對字的記憶；對大人來說，熟悉的玩具畫面與簡單的文字也使他們回味了。

  • 城市聲影

    interaction design pocessing arduino

    「城市聲影」這個裝置中， 我們以互動遊戲結合兒童學習的概念，運用微感測 器連接Arduino板，並透過Processing進行視覺效果 的呈現，創造出一個兼具互動性和趣味性的城市地 圖，讓兒童們在遊戲互動的過程中，喚醒他們對這 個城市的聲音記憶，重新認識這個城市的「聲影」 ，進而培養敏銳的觀察力。
